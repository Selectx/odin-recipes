Delicious Apple Pie

Description

This apple pie dump cake recipe was one of the first things I made when I was just learning to bake, and I still use it for parties as it is a crowd-pleaser.

Because it calls for only 3 ingredients, it is super quick and easy to make. A dollop of vanilla ice cream tops it very well.

Ingredients

  • 2 (21 ounce) cans apple pie filling
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • Â½ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Pour pie filling into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Sprinkle cake mix evenly over apple pie filling and top with melted butter.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the top starts to turn brown, about 40 minutes. Cool cake 15 minutes before serving.
